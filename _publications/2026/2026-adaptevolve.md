abstract: >-
  We propose AdaptEvolve, a framework for dynamic model routing in evolutionary agentic systems. 
  By bootstrapping an Adaptive Hoeffding Tree router with a 50-sample warm-up phase, we achieve 
  a 37.9% reduction in inference costs (FLOPS) while retaining 97.5% of the performance of 
  32B-parameter baselines on complex reasoning benchmarks.
