pub:            "Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing (EMNLP)"

abstract: >-
  We propose CSSL, a contrastive self-supervised learning framework for dependency parsing 
  in low-resource languages with relatively free word order and rich morphology. By applying 
  contrastive learning to Transformer encoders, we enable permutation invariance critical for 
  Indic language syntactic analysis.
